How To Play

  1. Click the CONNECT button to connect to the system!
  2. You will be given a passage you have to complete in 60 SECONDS order to win the game.
  3. If you are typing the word correctly, the text will turn green. The text will turn red, otherwise.
  4. There is also a light that you need to watch out for, and you are also given 5 hearts at the beginning of the game.
  5. When the light is GREEN, the system will allow you to type in the input.
  6. When the light is RED, the system will NOT allow you to type in the input.
    • If you type ANYTHING while the light is red, you will lose ONE heart. You lose when all your hearts are exhausted.
    • There are NO penalties for misspelling. You may type whatever as long as the light is green.
  7. And that's it!
